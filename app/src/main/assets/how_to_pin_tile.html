Pin the “Control Addiction” Notification Tile

1. Open Quick Settings

Swipe down from the top of your screen to fully expand the Quick Settings panel.

2. Tap the Edit Icon

Look for the pencil (✏️) or “Edit” icon and tap it to enter tile-edit mode.

3. Find the “Control Addiction” Tile

Scroll through the list of available tiles until you see the “Control Addiction” icon.

4. Drag & Drop

Long-press the “Control Addiction” tile and drag it up into the active panel area.

5. Enjoy One-Tap Access!

Now you can toggle your app rules directly from Quick Settings with a single tap.
